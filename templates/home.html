About Glassdoor

Glassdoor is a website where current and former employees anonymously review companies. It provides a platform for job seekers to explore company cultures, salary information, interview processes, and more. Employees can leave detailed reviews about their experiences working at a company, including the pros and cons, advice to management, and overall ratings. Users can find and share salary data for various positions within companies. This helps job seekers understand the market rate for different roles and negotiate their salaries effectively.
